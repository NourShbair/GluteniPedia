What is Gluten?

TGluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and related grains. It’s what gives dough its elasticity, allowing it to rise and maintain its shape, and gives bread its chewy texture. While gluten is a common component in many foods, it’s important to know that some people may have sensitivities or conditions like celiac disease that require them to avoid it. Whether you’re looking to learn more about gluten, manage a gluten-free diet, or simply explore gluten’s role in your food, our site offers valuable insights and resources to help you make informed choices.
